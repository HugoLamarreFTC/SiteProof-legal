English

Last updated: April 2026

SiteProof ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

1. No Account Required

SiteProof does not require you to create an account. You can use the app fully without providing any personal information.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All data created in SiteProof is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Photos you capture within the app
  • Job details (titles, notes, timestamps, locations)
  • Before and after comparisons
  • Generated reports and exports
  • Client signatures (if using sign-off features)

This data is not transmitted to any server and remains under your control.

3. No Cloud Storage

SiteProof does not upload your data to the cloud. The app does not operate any backend servers or remote databases.

4. No Tracking or Analytics

SiteProof does not use analytics tools, tracking SDKs, or advertising frameworks. We do not collect:

  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Behavioral data

5. Location Data

If enabled, SiteProof may access your device location to:

  • Tag job sites
  • Automatically populate addresses

Location data is used only on-device and is never transmitted or stored externally. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

6. Photos and Camera Access

SiteProof requests access to your camera and photo library to:

  • Capture before and after photos
  • Save generated images and reports

All images remain on your device unless you choose to share them.

7. Cryptographic Proof Features

SiteProof uses cryptographic functions to protect the integrity of your photos:

  • Photos are processed using cryptographic hash functions (SHA-256) to generate tamper-evident fingerprints
  • HMAC signatures are created using a device-local key stored securely in the iOS Keychain
  • All verification data is stored locally on your device

This process is performed entirely on-device. The cryptographic key never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external service.

8. In-App Purchases

SiteProof offers a one-time purchase to unlock Pro features. Payments are processed securely by Apple via the App Store. SiteProof does not have access to your payment information.

9. Data Sharing

SiteProof does not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties. Data is only shared when you explicitly choose to export or send files (e.g., via email, AirDrop, or other apps).

10. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting jobs within the app
  • Removing the app from your device

Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data, including any cryptographic keys stored in the Keychain.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

To the extent any data processing occurs, our legal bases are:

  • Consent: You grant device permissions (camera, location, photo library) voluntarily and can revoke them at any time in your device settings.
  • Legitimate interest: Providing core app functionality such as local data storage, cryptographic verification, and report generation.

12. Your Rights (EU/EEA Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and request portability of your personal data.

Because SiteProof stores all data locally on your device and has no access to it, you exercise these rights directly:

  • Access and portability: Use the app's export features to retrieve your data at any time.
  • Erasure: Delete individual jobs in the app, or delete the app entirely to remove all data.
  • Restriction and rectification: Edit or manage your data directly within the app.

If you have questions about exercising your rights, contact us at forthemcreation@gmail.com.

13. Children's Privacy

SiteProof is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

14. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

15. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Province of Quebec, Canada, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

16. Contact

SiteProof is developed and maintained by ForThemCreation. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: forthemcreation@gmail.com.

17. Website Hosting

This privacy policy page is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit this page, GitHub may process technical information such as IP address, browser details, and server request data as part of providing web hosting infrastructure.
